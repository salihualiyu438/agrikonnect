Privacy and Policy

Introduction

Agrikonnect is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

- Providing Services: We use your information to provide you with access to our platform and its features. - Improving Services: We use your information to improve our platform, including analyzing usage patterns and identifying areas for improvement. - Communication: We may use your information to communicate with you about our platform, including updates, promotions, and notifications.


Information sharing

- Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform. - Partners: We may share your information with partners who provide valuable services or information to our users. - Legal Requirements: We may share your information if required by law or to protect our rights and interests.


Data Security

- Encryption: We use encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. - Access Controls: We implement access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data. - Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures to ensure they remain effective.


Your Rights

- Access: You have the right to access your personal data and request corrections or updates. - Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. - Opt-out: You have the right to opt-out of marketing communications.
